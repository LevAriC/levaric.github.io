The RIBS-600 combines architectural presence with versatile lighting capabilities. Using an E27 light bulb socket, it offers flexibility in choosing the perfect illumination for your space.

RIBS-400

This versatile piece excels in multiple configurations:

  • Pairs or trios above kitchen islands
  • Single fixtures in reading corners
  • Above couches
  • Hallways and entryways
