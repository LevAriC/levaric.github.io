RiBS by Omer Zimmermann

In the quiet hours of a winter evening, Omer Zimmermann sat in his workshop, contemplating the interplay of light and shadow. Inspired by the elegant curves of architectural ribbing and the way light dances through organic forms, he envisioned something extraordinary ‚Äì a light fixture that would breathe life into any space. The "RiBS" collection was born from this moment of pure creative inspiration.

Each piece in the RiBS series tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship and innovative design. Omer spent months perfecting the balance between form and function, ensuring that every curve catches and redirects light in the most captivating way. These aren't just light fixtures; they're sculptural statements that transform ordinary rooms into spaces of wonder and warmth.
