RiBS Collection by Omer Zimmermann

In the quiet hours of a winter evening, Omer Zimmermann sat in his workshop, contemplating the interplay of light and shadow. Inspired by the elegant curves of architectural ribbing and the way light dances through organic forms, he envisioned something extraordinary ‚Äì a light fixture that would breathe life into any space. The "RiBS" collection was born from this moment of pure creative inspiration.

Each piece in the RiBS series tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship and innovative design. Omer spent months perfecting the balance between form and function, ensuring that every curve catches and redirects light in the most captivating way. These aren't just light fixtures; they're sculptural statements that transform ordinary rooms into spaces of wonder and warmth.

Explore the different variations of the RiBS collection using the navigation above, or browse the main collection below.

RiBS - Long by Omer Zimmermann

The extended version of the RiBS collection, featuring elongated forms that create dramatic lighting effects across larger spaces. These pieces are perfect for dining areas, hallways, and spaces where you want to make a bold statement with light.

RiBS - Short by Omer Zimmermann

The compact version of the RiBS collection, designed for intimate spaces and accent lighting. These pieces provide focused illumination while maintaining the distinctive aesthetic of the RiBS design language.

RiBS - Wall by Omer Zimmermann

Wall-mounted versions of the RiBS collection that transform vertical surfaces into canvases of light and shadow. These pieces are perfect for creating dramatic focal points and ambient lighting.

Collaboration with Avi Ben Shoshan

A unique creative partnership between Omer Zimmermann and renowned architect Avi Ben Shoshan, exploring the intersection of architectural design and innovative lighting solutions. This collaboration merges Ben Shoshan's visionary spatial concepts with Zimmermann's mastery of light, creating installations that transform environments and redefine the relationship between structure and illumination.

Through this partnership, architectural elements become canvases for light, while lighting fixtures evolve into structural components. The result is a seamless integration where design and function unite to create spaces that are both visually striking and deeply atmospheric, showcasing the power of collaborative creativity in contemporary design.
